General First Aid (AVMA/WSAVA): For bleeding, apply pressure; vomiting—withhold food, see vet. In emergencies, stabilize and go to nearest vet.

Sri Lanka-Specific: For rabies exposure, wash wound, vaccinate immediately via DAPH clinic. Tick fever: Remove ticks, monitor fever; Anuradhapura has Veterinary Investigation Centers for testing.

Emergencies: Poisoning (e.g., chocolate)—induce vomiting if recent, call vet. Heatstroke common in hot areas: Cool pet, provide water.
