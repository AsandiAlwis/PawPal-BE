Core Vaccines for Dogs (Global/WSAVA): CDV, CAV, CPV, Rabies (essential in Sri Lanka due to endemic status). Start at 6-8 weeks, boosters every 2-4 weeks until 16 weeks, then every 3 years for low-risk.

Sri Lanka-Specific (Indira Silva/Partial): Follow WSAVA but adapt for local rabies/tick fever. Core: Rabies (annual), Parvovirus, Distemper. Non-core: Leptospirosis if in wet areas like North Central Province. Vaccination is active immunization; schedule for puppies: 6-8 weeks first dose, boosters to 16 weeks.

AVMA Schedule: Puppies 6-8 weeks (CPV, CDV, CAV), rabies at 12 weeks. Adults: Boosters 1-3 years based on risk.

DAPH Notes (Sri Lanka): National vaccine distribution for rabies/endemic diseases; surveillance for zoonotics like rabies. In Anuradhapura, contact local Veterinary Investigation Center for free rabies shots.

Preventive: Annual health checks; tick control for fever prevalence in Sri Lanka.
