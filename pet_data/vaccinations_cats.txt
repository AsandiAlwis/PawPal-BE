Core Vaccines for Cats (WSAVA): FPV, FCV, FHV, Rabies (endemic in Sri Lanka). Start at 6-8 weeks, boosters to 16 weeks, then every 3 years.

AVMA: Same cores; FeLV non-core for outdoor cats. Kittens: 6-8 weeks start, rabies at 12 weeks.

Preventive: Indoor lifestyle reduces FeLV/FIV risk; annual checks for zoonotics.
